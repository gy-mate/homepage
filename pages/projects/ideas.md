## Phone Street View

Estimated cost: 650.000 HUF (€1700)

1. Write a BeeWare app that can
    - in record mode: synchronize time using NTP (e.g. https://github.com/jsiembida/python-ntp), geotag the images and async send them to a device on the local network 
    - in receive mode: merge, store and show the images—it also uploads them to Panoramax upon acceptance
1. Engineer a phone holder similar to [V6MPack](https://wiki.openstreetmap.org/w/images/4/4f/20231111_-_Centimeter_geolocation_at_low_cost_with_Centipede_RTK.pdf#page=5) 
   but with holes for MagSafe power banks
1. Wait until used iPhone 16 Pros or 17s or Pixel 8 Pros become super cheap (<75.000 HUF; around 2027)
1. Buy 5 of them (~500.000 HUF)
1. Buy 6 Magsafe power banks (~150.000 HUF): https://www.alza.hu/alzapower-qi2-ultra-slim-5000-mah-power-delivery-20-w-sotetkek-d12805537.htm
1. Mount the phones
